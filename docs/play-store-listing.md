## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Quotr is the all-in-one business app for contractors, tradespeople, and freelancers.**

Stop losing receipts in your van. Stop chasing payments. Stop guessing your profit margins. Quotr gives you everything you need to run your business from your phone.

**INVOICE IN SECONDS**
Create professional invoices on-site and send them instantly via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. Your clients get a polished PDF with your logo and branding. Support for Irish VAT rates (23%, 13.5%, 9%, 0%) built in.

**SCAN RECEIPTS WITH AI**
Just snap a photo of any receipt. Our AI automatically extracts the vendor, amount, date, and items. No more shoeboxes full of crumpled paper at tax time.

**TRACK YOUR TIME**
Built-in timer for logging billable hours. Start the clock when you arrive at a job, stop when you leave. Convert time entries directly into invoice line items.

**MANAGE CLIENTS**
Keep all your client details in one place - contacts, addresses, email, phone. Import directly from your phone's contacts. See payment history at a glance.

**CREATE QUOTES**
Win more work with professional quotes. Convert accepted quotes to invoices with one tap. Track quote status from sent to converted.

**MATERIALS TRACKING**
Log materials purchased for each job. Know exactly what you spent on supplies and bill your clients accurately.

**BUSINESS INSIGHTS**
See your weekly revenue, expenses, and profit at a glance. VAT summaries ready for your accountant. Client revenue breakdowns to spot your best customers.

**TEAM COLLABORATION**
Growing your business? Invite team members with role-based access. Owners, admins, staff, and viewers - everyone sees only what they need.

**CUSTOMISE YOUR BRAND**
Upload your logo. Choose your brand colours. Pick from three professional invoice templates. Add custom terms and payment details.

**WORKS OFFLINE**
Network issues on a rural job site? No problem. Quotr works offline and syncs when you're back online.

**DESIGNED FOR IRELAND**
Euro currency, Irish VAT rates, DD/MM/YYYY dates. Built by a tradesperson, for tradespeople.
